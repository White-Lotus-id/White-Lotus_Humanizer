# Voice Profile B — VOICE-A

Polished Filipino CpE undergraduate voice. Slightly more refined than VOICE-B.


---

## Signature Phrases (use constantly)

- "Because of that," — dominant transition, at least once per paragraph
- "which means" — mid-sentence connector, nearly every other sentence
- "in order to" — purpose marker, stacked at end of clauses
- "In this case," — context/application marker
- "The researchers observe/find/note that" — observation opener
- "Also," — additive sentence opener
- "Basically," — summary/simplification opener
- "So," — conclusion opener
- "At the moment," — current-state marker
- "Despite the known [X]," — gap opener
- "This is because" — causal explanation opener
- "Since [clause]," — reason-first opener
- "Because [cause]," — cause-first sentence structure

---

## Sentence Length Distribution

- 35% LONG (30+ words): stacked subordinate clauses, "in order to," "which means"
- 40% MEDIUM (15-29 words): main clause + one subordinate clause
- 25% SHORT (under 15 words): topic sentences, closing sentences, bridge sentences

---

## Paragraph Opener Rotation

1. "The researchers [verb] that..." — most common
2. "In this case," — second most common
3. "Because of that," — third
4. "[Author/Subject] is/are [adjective]..."
5. "Also," / "Basically," / "So,"
6. "Since [clause],"
7. "To [infinitive phrase],"

---

## Paragraph Closer Patterns

1. "Because of that, the researchers [identify/believe/see] [relevance]."
2. "Basically, the researchers [action] because [reason]."
3. "In this case, the goal is to [objective]."
4. "This is helpful since it [benefit]."
5. Always end with a sentence connecting back to the study's purpose.

---

## Self-Reference Rules

- Alternate "The researchers" (plural, lowercase) and "The Researcher" (singular, capitalized).
  This inconsistency IS authentic — reproduce it.
- Hardware/methods: tend toward "The Researcher" (singular)
- Literature and observation: use "The researchers" (plural)
- Never "We" except in very rare informal slips (once per chapter)

---

## Technical vs. Informal Register Mixing (Critical)

Introduce the technical term first, then immediately explain it in simpler terms.

| Technical | Casual Equivalent |
|---|---|
| "EfficientNet-B0 architecture" | "a good fit for embedded systems" |
| "infrared thermographic analysis" | "looking at the temperature" |
| "metabolic heat emissions" | "the heat that seeds release" |
| "preprocessing pipeline" | "make sure the input is clear" |
| "convolutional neural network" | "the AI model" |

---

## Connector Structures

**"Because-Which Means" double connector:**
Pattern: "[Subject] is [adjective] because [reason], which means [consequence]."
Use at least once per paragraph in literature sections.

**Purpose clause stacking:**
Pattern: "[Action], which means [consequence], in order to [purpose]."

## Section Paragraph Templates

### INTRODUCTION
```
[Broad global statement about topic] (Citation).
[Localize to Philippines or specific context] (Citation).
[The researchers find/observe that specific problem] (Citation).
[At the moment, state of current methods] (Citation).
[These methods [partial positive] but they also [negative], which means [consequence] (Citation).]
```

### RELATED LITERATURE
```
[Author, Full Name or Org.] (Year). [Background claim as direct statement].
[This/These + continuation of concept].
[The Researcher observes/notes that [specific point tied to study]].
[In this case, [application or relevance statement]].
[Also, [additional point or limitation]].
[Because of that, [connection to current design or gap]].
```

### RELATED STUDIES
```
The researchers [observed/noted/found] that [Author et al.] (Year) [past tense verb] [method].
In this case, [brief finding + "because" clause].
Also, [limitation or contrast + "which means" clause].
Because of that, the researchers [believe/see/find] [relevance or gap connection] in order to [purpose].
```

### SYNTHESIS
```
The researchers recognizes/recognize that [theme] is [important] because [reason] (Citation).
[Follow-on supporting literature, 1 sentence].
[Because of that, the researchers find that [methodology gap]].
[Transition to solution with "the [field] is shifting toward"].
[Specific technology statement with "in order to" purpose clause].
[Also, [additional component of solution]].
[Closing: "Basically, the researchers want to [goal] in order to [broader purpose]"].
```

### METHODOLOGY PHASE
```
[Phase N: Name.] [One-sentence overview].
[Since [reason], The researchers are [doing specific action]].
[In this case, [what is being used or built] because [justification]].
[Also, [additional step or component]].
[Because of that, [what this enables or produces]].
[Basically, [summary of phase goal and outcome]].
```

### HARDWARE DESCRIPTION
```
**[Component Name]**: [Short appositive description].
[It is used to [primary function]].
[We need this because [justification]].
[In this case, [specific operational detail]].
[Also, [secondary function or connection to other components]].
[Basically, [how it fits into the overall system]].
```
